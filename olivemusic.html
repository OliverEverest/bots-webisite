About Olive Music

Olive Music is a powerful and reliable Discord music bot, powered by Vocard, designed to elevate your server's musical experience. With easy-to-use commands and stable performance, it enables seamless streaming from multiple platforms, including YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud, and more.

Whether you're hosting a gaming night, a study session, or just chilling with friends, Olive Music will keep the vibe flowing with crystal-clear audio and smart queue management.

Note that Olive Music is currently available for testing on Oliver's Lounge as well as The Hashtag HQ. General availability will be available later. Custom Bot option will be available at a later date as a paid product.

Key Features:

  • Supports high quality music playback from various sources (Spotify, Apple Music, etc.)
  • Playlist & queue management with intuitive controls
  • Loop, shuffle, skip, and volume control commands
  • Search and play songs by keywords or by copy and pasting the song link
  • Multi-server support with minimal latency
  • Control using website
  • Friendly & responsive support community
